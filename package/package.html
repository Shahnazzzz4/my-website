Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our travel packages

What is included in the package price?
Our packages typically include accommodation, breakfast, transportation between destinations, guided tours, and entry tickets to major attractions. Some packages may also include select meals and activities. Please check individual package details for specific inclusions.
Can I customize the package according to my preferences?
Yes, absolutely! We understand that every traveler is unique. You can customize any package by adding extra days, changing hotels, including additional activities, or modifying the itinerary. Contact our travel consultants to discuss your requirements.
What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellation policies vary based on the package and booking date. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before travel receive a full refund (minus processing fees). Between 15-30 days, a 50% refund applies. Cancellations within 15 days are non-refundable. Please refer to the terms and conditions during booking.
Do you provide travel insurance?
While basic travel insurance is recommended, we can assist you in purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and baggage loss. Our team will help you choose the right insurance plan for your trip.
What are the payment options available?
We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, UPI, and digital wallets. You can pay a booking amount (typically 25-30%) to confirm your reservation, with the balance payable 15 days before travel.
Are flights included in the package?
Package prices typically do not include international or domestic flights unless specifically mentioned. However, we can help you book flights at competitive rates. Our team will provide flight options and assist with the booking process.
