Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I book a travel package?

You can book a travel package through our website, by calling us at +91 12345 67890, or by sending us an email at support@travelindia.com. Our travel experts will guide you through the entire booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and cash payments. For international travelers, we also accept PayPal and bank transfers.

Can I customize my travel itinerary?

Yes, absolutely! We specialize in creating customized travel itineraries tailored to your preferences, budget, and schedule. Contact our travel experts to discuss your requirements.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options to protect your trip. Our team will help you choose the best insurance plan based on your travel needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by package type. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before travel receive a full refund minus processing fees. Please contact us for specific cancellation terms related to your booking.

How do I get travel documents and vouchers?

Once your booking is confirmed, we will send all travel documents, vouchers, and itineraries to your registered email address. You can also access them through your account on our website.
